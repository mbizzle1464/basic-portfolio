About Me

I started coding when I was promoted to the current role I am in now. I started working with html emails and became intimate with understaing the art of html/css. I absolutely love working with code and decided to commit myself to this craft. I am currently placed in SMU's Full Stack Web Development Part-time course to take myself to the next level.
